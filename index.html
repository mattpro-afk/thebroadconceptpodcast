Who We Are

Building a Generation of
Change-Makers

The Broad Concept Group (BCG) is an emerging social-impact organisation dedicated to empowering young people and communities through education, climate action, technology & innovation, and communication skills.

We believe: if you want to go fast, go alone — if you want to go far, go together. Our ecosystem nurtures youth from primary school to adulthood, ensuring no learner is left behind and that impact is continuous, compounding, and generational.

What We Stand For

Our Four Strategic Pillars

📚

Education

Access to quality learning, scholarships, foundational soft skills, and exposure to world-changing ideas for underserved communities.

🌍

Climate Action

Climate clubs, tree-planting, youth climate projects, and awareness campaigns equipping communities with environmental stewardship.

🎙️

Communication for Impact

Public speaking, digital storytelling, advocacy, and ESG communication — teaching youth to speak, lead, and amplify change.

💡

Technology & Innovation

AI literacy, digital skills, STEM exposure, and "Tech for Good" challenges preparing youth for a rapidly evolving world.
